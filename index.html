Digital Fashion Presentation

Created by George Mutambuka (TheGiwi)
Digital fashion is not about replacing the physical but it is about enhancing a fashion brand's presence in the world through interactive fashion and visual design.

High fashion concepts can be created with various iterations and modifications.
From these ideas people can make physical orders using some elements from the digital collection.

3D digitization of physical outfits can also be created as a way of archiving a fashion brand's collections.
Especially if a limited number of an outfit were created and they were all sold leaving no sample that can be seen.

A 3D model also has the benefit of being viewed from all possible angles, an advantage which normal photographs don't have.
In the following examples I decided to take a sample from of a real dress and digitally recreate it in various digital presentations.
